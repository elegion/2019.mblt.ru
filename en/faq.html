FAQ

I didn’t receive a ticket. What shall I do?

— The ticket is sent to the email address indicated at your RUNET-ID account. It won’t be delivered to the email address of a person who proceeds with the payment. Please check out the correct mailbox.
— If the mailbox is correct, please check the “Spam” folder.
— If there is no ticket, please go to your RUNET-ID account. Go to the “My events” tab, find the MBLT19 there and check if you have paid for the ticket. Your status should be changed from the “Virtual participant” to the “Participant”.
— If the payment has been completed and you still haven’t got your ticket, please contact the RUNET-ID Financial service via email fin@ruvents.com or phone +7 (495) 950-56-51.

How can legal entities pay for the ticket?

Press the “Buy Tickets” button → “Go to your profile” (if you have no account, please register here → “Proceed to Payment”. While proceeding with the registration in your account, please choose the “For legal entities” option → issue an invoice. If you have any difficulties with the payment or documents, please contact the RUNET-ID Financial service via email fin@ruvents.com or phone +7 (495) 950-56-51.

Please help me to convince my boss!

We have prepared a a letter template to convince your boss that you should visit the MBLT19.

How can I join the Conference for free?

There are several options:
1). Become the Conference volunteer. We are searching for photographs, registration admins and assistants who are going to communicate with the speakers and audience while during the talks. Please email to Kristina Mazurenko via km@e-legion.com and briefly tell about yourself and describe how you could help us.
2). Become the Conference Media Partner. Do you have a relevant media source or channel where you could share the MBLT19 news? Please contact us via marketing@e-legion.com.
3). Become a speaker. Do you have some interesting point to share with the mobile app developers community? Please fill in the form to submit a paper.

What shall I bring with me to the conference?

We recommend taking your laptop so that you could easily participate in different coding activities and take some notes while attending the talks. Don’t forget to take your gadgets chargers. Please also print the ticket or be ready to show it on your smartphone screen at the registration desk.

How can I address my questions to the conference speakers?

There will be a 5-7 min QA session in the Hall right after the talk. The special discussion zone will be also working so you will have a chance to ask your questions face-to-face.
